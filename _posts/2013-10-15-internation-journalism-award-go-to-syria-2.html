--- layout: post title: Want to win an international journalistic award? Go to Syria. ---

The 20th edition of the Bayeux Prize for war correspondents is just over, after the awards were given.
Some great and important reportages were selected, but the praises to their authors can't help the journalistic field to forget the price which was payed for these informations. "Never a year was so macabre", says the Bayeux blog. It's no big news: war kills; but what make the Bayeux assembly cry, is that journalists are now targets in the conflicts.

Unsurprisingly, Syria has a front seat among the countries chosen by the war reporters gathered in Bayeux. Deadly conflit that nothing seems to be able to stop, and that some say reinforced by the recent Nobel peace prize.
